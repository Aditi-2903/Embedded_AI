# Chapter 2 — Embedded Constraints as Design Variables

> *The student learns to quantify embedded system constraints in terms that make AI integration decisions computable.*

**Part I — The Embedded-AI Problem**

---

## Learning Outcomes

- **LO2.1** [Apply] Specify the memory, compute, power, and timing constraints of a given embedded target
- **LO2.2** [Analyze] Translate datasheet specifications into design constraints relevant to AI deployment
- **LO2.3** [Evaluate] Determine whether a proposed deployment target is feasible before selecting a model

---

> **Note for students arriving with embedded background:** This chapter reframes what you already know. The content is not new — the framing is. Constraint quantification is the language the rest of this book uses to make decisions.
